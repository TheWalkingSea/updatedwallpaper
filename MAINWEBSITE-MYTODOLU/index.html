    About CanvList

    CanvList provides an easy and automatic way to check for incomplete assignments by setting your wallpaper. It's automatic and secure design prevents any issues.

    CanvList formats it's list into 3 separate columns: assignments, points, and due date. It is further sorted by course name, listing each assignment. The background picture, font, and layout are fully customizable by the user.
